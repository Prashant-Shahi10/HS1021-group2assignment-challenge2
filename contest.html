Photo Contest

Show off your photography skills by entering our monthly photo contest! It's a great opportunity to get your work seen, win prizes, and connect with other photographers.

Contest Categories

We have several exciting categories for you to choose from each month:

  • Child Photos: Capture the innocence and joy of children.
  • Flower Photos: Highlight the beauty and detail of flowers.
  • Scenic Photos: Share stunning landscapes and scenic views.

How to Enter

Entering is easy:

  1. Select your best photo for each category you want to enter.
  2. Ensure your photo is in JPEG format and meets our size requirements.
  3. Fill out the entry form with your name, contact information, and photo details.
  4. Email your entry to Gerry Hayward at gerry@camshots.com.

Judging and Prizes

Our panel of experienced judges will review all entries and select the top three photos in each category based on creativity, technical skill, and overall impact. Winners will be featured on our website and receive exclusive prizes like photography gear and software.
